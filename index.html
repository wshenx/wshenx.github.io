Wei Shen

I joined Tencent in 2019. Before that I was a researcher in Fujitsu R&D center in Beijing from 2016. I received my Ph.D. degree from Institute of Automation Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2016, under the supervision of Prof. Jie Tian. I received the B.S. degree in Hydropower in Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2011.

My research interests include image manipulation, generative model, classification, meta-learning, etc.
